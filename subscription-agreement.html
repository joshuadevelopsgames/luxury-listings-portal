1 Services

Provider will make available to Customer a web-based software platform for luxury real estate marketers, enabling users to manage clients through a built-in CRM, create and run listing promotion and posting packages, and coordinate team workflow and reporting (the "Platform"). The Platform is provided on a software-as-a-service (SaaS) basis and is accessible via login credentials issued by Provider. Provider will host the Platform and provide standard technical support as described in Section 13.

2.2 Prohibited Conduct. Customer must not, and must ensure that its users do not:

(a) use the Platform for any unlawful purpose or in violation of any applicable law or regulation;
(b) attempt to reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or derive source code from the Platform;
(c) attempt to gain unauthorized access to any part of the Platform, its related systems, or the accounts of other users;

(g) sublicense, resell, or make the Platform available to any third party without Provider's prior written consent; or
(h) collect, process, or store personal information through the Platform in violation of applicable privacy laws, including PIPEDA and the Personal Information Protection Act (BC).

Such suspension does not limit any other remedy available to Provider under this Agreement or at law.

3 Subscription Fees & Pricing

3.1 Base Fee. Customer will pay a flat monthly base fee of $200.00 CAD per month.

3.2 Per-User Fee. In addition to the base fee, Customer will pay $20.00 CAD per active user per month. For purposes of this Agreement, an "active user" means any user account provisioned by Provider at Customer's written request and active in the Platform as of the billing date for the applicable month.

3.3 Price Changes. Provider will provide Customer with at least 30 days' written notice of any changes to pricing or to how active users are counted. Price changes take effect on the first day of the billing period following the end of the notice period.

4 Billing & Payment

4.1 Billing Cycle. Fees are billed monthly in advance. Invoices are issued on the 1st of each calendar month and are due Net 15 (i.e., payable within 15 days of the invoice date).

4.2 Auto-Renewal. This Agreement and the Customer's subscription will automatically renew on a month-to-month basis unless terminated in accordance with Section 8.

4.3 Payment Method. Payment is to be made by Interac e-Transfer to jrsschroeder@gmail.com. It is Customer's responsibility to ensure transfers are sent to the correct address by the due date.

4.4 Late Payments and Suspension. Amounts not paid by the due date will accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month (approximately 18% per annum), calculated from the due date until the date payment is received in full. Provider will follow the escalation process below for overdue amounts:

(a) Reminder Notice. If an invoice remains unpaid 15 days after the due date, Provider will send Customer a written reminder by email. Late fees begin accruing from the original due date regardless of when the reminder is sent.
(b) Suspension Warning. If the invoice remains unpaid 30 days after the due date, Provider will issue a formal suspension warning giving Customer 14 days to bring the account current.
(c) Service Suspension. If Customer has two or more consecutive unpaid invoices, or if any single invoice remains unpaid for more than 45 days past its due date, Provider may suspend Customer's access to the Platform without further notice until all outstanding amounts (including accrued interest) are paid in full. Suspension does not relieve Customer of its obligation to pay outstanding fees.
(d) Termination for Non-Payment. If Customer's account remains suspended for more than 30 days, Provider may terminate this Agreement upon written notice. For greater certainty, this Section 4.4 governs the process for termination due to non-payment. In the event of any conflict between this Section 4.4 and Section 8.3, this Section 4.4 prevails.
5 User Management & True-Up

5.1 Provider-Managed Access. Customer does not have direct access to the Platform's administrative panel. All user provisioning is managed exclusively by Provider. To add or remove a user, Customer must submit a written request to Provider by email at jrsschroeder@gmail.com. Provider will process the request within one business day of receipt and confirm by reply email once the change has been made.

5.2 Billing for User Changes. User additions will be reflected on the invoice for the month in which the user was activated. User removals will take effect at the end of the current billing month and will be reflected on the following month's invoice. Provider will maintain an accurate record of all active users and will provide Customer with a current user summary upon request.
